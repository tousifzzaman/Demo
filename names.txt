


akele kitne main ne raate gujara hain
sabko sath leke chalna caha mein
koi bola ha toh koi bola na
aakhir mein khudko akela hi paya main






woh toh samajh ta hain ki hum toh bekar hain
isiliye unka itna khayal rakhte hain
lekin unko kon samjhaye 
ki hum toh sirf unko hi cahte hain 



humko toh nhi aata jaam uthana
yhi toh jariya hain gam bhulana
likh leta hun isliye ki ghat jaaye yeh gaam
lekin aakhir mein reh hi jata hain aankh main naam...




samajhne wale samajh gaye honge
samajh ke bhi bhul gaye honge 
unko kya hi pata ki unke liye kitne roye honge…….
